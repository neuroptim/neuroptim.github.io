Neuromorphic Optimization Research

Centralizing collaborative efforts in neuromorphic computing and optimization

About Us

NeurOptim is a research initiative dedicated to advancing the field of neuromorphic optimization. We bring together researchers and collaborators from various institutions to tackle the challenges of developing brain-inspired computing systems and optimization algorithms.

Our goal is to centralize all efforts from different collaborators in the field, fostering collaboration and accelerating innovation in neuromorphic computing.

Research Areas

Neuromorphic Hardware

Developing and optimizing brain-inspired hardware architectures for efficient computation.

Spiking Neural Networks

Advancing algorithms and architectures for spiking neural networks and event-driven computing.

Optimization Algorithms

Creating novel optimization methods inspired by neuromorphic principles and biological systems.
