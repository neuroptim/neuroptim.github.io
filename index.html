About Us

NeurOptim is a research initiative dedicated to advancing the field of neuromorphic optimization. We bring together researchers and collaborators from various institutions to tackle the challenges of developing brain-inspired computing systems and optimization algorithms.

Our goal is to centralize all efforts from different collaborators in the field, fostering collaboration and accelerating innovation in neuromorphic computing.
